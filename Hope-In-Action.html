When Ahmed Hassan collapsed from blood loss at PIMS Hospital, our AI system found a compatible O+ donor in 90 seconds.

The donor, just 3 kilometers away, received an instant notification. Within 2 hours, Ahmed was receiving the blood that saved his life. This is the power of technology meeting compassion.
